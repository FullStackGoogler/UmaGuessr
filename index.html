How to Play

You have 6 attempts to guess the Uma Musume. Skipping or submitting an incorrect guess reveals an additional bit of information about the Uma.


NPCs and/or unreleased Uma Musume are not included. Some Umas may also only have their hints in Japanese at the moment. Some Umas are also not included because they are missing bits of information, which I will try and slowly add in!


Note: Only dailies count towards the Stats, and are stored locally.
